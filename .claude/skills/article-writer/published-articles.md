# Published Articles — Topic & Pattern Ledger

The article-writer skill MUST read this file before proposing a topic, and MUST append a one-line entry after every successful publish.

## Burned patterns — do NOT reuse

If your draft uses any of the items below, rewrite. Add a new item here whenever a new pattern reaches saturation; remove an item once enough articles have shipped without it.

- **Title shape "The Hidden Cost of [Manual X]"** — saturated.
- **Title shape "Where [stat]% of X [verb]"** — saturated.
- **Headline number 80%** as title / excerpt / seoDescription anchor — saturated. Body mention with a source is fine.
- **Phrase "quietly lose [Y]"** (any "X quietly lose Y" construction).
- **Phrase "what the same desk looks like once AI handles the repetitive/repeatable 80%"**.
- **Self-coined "[noun] gap"** branding — already used once ("the follow-up gap"), do not coin another.
- **Phrase "never says no — just goes silent"** in any form.

## Title-shape diversity rule

Across the most recent 5 articles, no more than one may share the same syntactic template. Pick a shape the recent 5 do NOT use.

## Headline-number diversity rule

Each new article picks its own anchor stat. Don't reuse a previously-featured headline number unless it's genuinely the central, sourced figure for the new topic.
