  1. Gear icon ⚙ → Import data → Invoices
  2. Upload the downloaded CSV, then map the columns (they're named to match)
  3. Review and import. Up to 100 invoices per file.
One-time setup: the Customer names and Item names in the file must match your QBO exactly, or those rows are rejected. Credits and payments are in the checklist below — QBO's importer can't take those.

Enter by hand · Payments

Rule: a check with a number → record it (and apply its credit if it has one); cash → record it; no check number → leave the invoice open. The check number is read from the sheet's Check column — but always confirm the method against the paper, since the PDF can't reliably tell cash apart from an open invoice.

Skipped (not entered)

Two kinds, neither billable: the PDF's route/week summary pages, and any stop whose header isn't red (a "not delivered" sheet — blue/cyan). Listed so you can confirm every page was accounted for. If a skipped stop ever shows a dollar amount, check it against the paper.
